# 3Speak

3Speak is a video hosting and sharing platform built on the Hive blockchain. Here we'll explore all the components available under **HiveFlutterKit** for 3Speak, including video feeds, video player, and search. All of them are available as widgets that can be easily integrated into your Flutter applications. We've added screenshots to help you visualize how these components look and function. And for each component, we've a separate page with detailed information.

### 3Speak - Video Feeds

This widget displays different types of video feeds. You can customize the feed type and handle taps on video items.
